Everything Your Farm Needs

A complete dairy management system, from cow to cash. Built for real-world farm operations.

Cow Management

Complete cow profiles with breed (Friesian, Ayrshire, Jersey, Guernsey, Sahiwal), age, lactation status, and medical history. Full CRUD with search and advanced filters.

Milk Recording

Record AM and PM yields per cow daily. Auto-calculates daily, weekly, and monthly totals. Visualize production trends with interactive Chart.js graphs.

Financial Ledger

Comprehensive income (milk sales, calf sales, grants) and expense tracking (feed, vet, labor, utilities). Real-time running balance and profit/loss reports.

Health Events

Log vaccinations, treatments, deworming, calving, and illness episodes. Attach vet costs automatically to financial transactions. Health history timeline per cow.

Task Reminders

Schedule tasks with due dates, priority levels (high/medium/low), and assign to staff. Dashboard shows upcoming and overdue tasks. Email notifications coming soon.

Employee Records

Manage farm workers, roles (milker, vet, feeder), salaries, contact info, and hire dates. Track total monthly payroll and individual performance.

PDF & CSV Reports

Generate professional milk production reports, financial summaries, and health logs. Download as PDF with custom farm branding or CSV for Excel analysis.

Mobile Responsive

How It Works

From setup to insights — get started in under 10 minutes.

Register Your Farm

Create your account with farm name, location, and herd size. Django authentication keeps your data secure with enterprise-grade encryption.

Add Your Cows

Enter each cow's ear tag number, breed, date of birth, and current status. Import multiple cows via CSV template for bulk setup.

Record Daily Yields

Log AM and PM milk yields each day in liters or kilograms. System auto-calculates totals, averages, and identifies top producers.

Track & Report

View real-time dashboard metrics, generate PDF reports for stakeholders, export CSV data for advanced analysis. Make data-driven decisions.
